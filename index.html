The Weekend Details

Wine & Welcome

Thursday, June 5 from 6PM to 9PM
Roche Winery, Sonoma CA

We’re so excited to kick off our wedding weekend with a welcome party at Roche Winery! Join us for an evening of great wine, delicious appetizers, lawn games, and time to mingle before the big day.

We’ll have a paella pop-up as a hearty appetizer to keep things casual, festive, and full of flavor.

Wedding Ceremony & Reception

Friday, June 6 from 5:30PM to midnight
Jacuzzi Vineyards, Sonoma CA

Join us for an unforgettable evening as we exchange vows and celebrate with dinner, dancing, and plenty of joy.

A shuttle will be available on the wedding day to transport guests between Sonoma Plaza and Jacuzzi Vineyards, both before and after the night. There is ample parking at the venue, and cars can be left overnight until 10 AM the following day. Ubers/Lyfts are difficult to obtain at night and in Sonoma, so please plan transportation accordingly.

After the reception, we’ll be heading to Steiner's Tavern in Sonoma Plaza to keep the celebration going. If you’re up for an unhosted nightcap, we’d love for you to join.

Farewell Coffee

Saturday, June 7
Sonoma Plaza
10:00 to 11:30AM

We’ll be hanging out in the plaza park with coffee in hand—feel free to swing by, say goodbye, and soak in a little more Sonoma sunshine with us.

What to Wear

(But Make It Yours)

These are vibes to guide you, but feel free to embrace your own style! We just want you there to celebrate with us, so wear whatever makes you feel great and comfortable.

Welcome Party: Vineyard Chic
Summery and stylish with a relaxed wine country feel.

  • Women: Sundresses, midi skirts, or tailored pants with a stylish blouse. Bring a light shawl or jacket for the evening.
  • Men: Lightweight button-downs or polos with chinos, tailored shorts, or dark jeans.

Wedding Attire: Colorful Black Tie
A formal evening with a fun, colorful twist!

  • Women: Full-length gowns or dresses in bold or rich colors.
  • Men: Black tuxedos preferred, or dark suits. Add colorful ties, pocket squares, or fun accessories to embrace the celebratory spirit.

Farewell Coffee: Summer Casual
Easygoing and comfortable for a sunny morning in the park.
