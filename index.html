Accommodations in Sonoma

We suggest booking lodging early to secure the best prices. Below are our hotel recommendations, though Airbnb or Vrbo are great alternatives. For convenience, we recommend staying north of Jacuzzi Vineyards to avoid unpredictable traffic.

A shuttle will be available on the wedding day to transport guests between Sonoma Plaza and Jacuzzi Vineyards, both before and after the night. There is ample parking at the venue, and cars can be left overnight until 10 AM the following day. Ubers/Lyfts are difficult to obtain at night and in Sonoma, so please plan transportation accordingly.

Take Me to California

Traveling directly to Sonoma from the West Coast, we recommend looking into flights to Charles M. Schulz Sonoma County Airport in Santa Rosa supported by Alaska & American Airlines.

Traveling directly to Sonoma from the East Coast, we recommend flying into Oakland International Airport located an hour and 15 minutes from Sonoma, pending traffic times.

Traveling to San Francisco first, we recommend flying into San Francisco International Airport located an hour and 30 minutes from Sonoma, pending traffic times.

Beginning May 7, 2025, every traveler 18 years of age or older will need a REAL ID-compliant driver's license at airport security checkpoints for domestic air travel.
